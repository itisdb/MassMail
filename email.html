Call for Paper

 

Dear Authors,

This is to invite you to submit high-quality research papers in the “International Symposium on Intelligent IoT & Edge Computing” will be held on 11th-13th November  2021 at IEM Kolkata.

In this forum, researchers, students and academicians can showcase their original, novel ideas in the field of intelligent IoT and Edge computing.

We hereby invite papers presenting original research on the topic and hope to receive many high-quality submissions.

Submission deadline of abstracts is 15/08/21

 

Research articles, review articles as well as communications are invited. Submitted manuscripts should not have been published previously, nor be under consideration for publication elsewhere. All manuscripts are referred through a peer-review process. Selected papers will be published in Scopus Indexed proceedings which is yet to be confirmed(follow the website).  

The papers should be submitted via https://easychair.org . The Paper submission guidelines are mentioned in our website:https://isiiec.org and the page limit is maximum 8 pages. Kindly contact us through our official email id: conference.isiiec@gmail.com,convenor@isiiec.org .
